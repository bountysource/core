Fees & Payments

TL;DR No fees on spending or earning. 10% fee to withdraw.

Spending Money

There are no fees associated with posting a bounty, pledging a fundraiser, or gifting to a team. All payment processing fees are covered by Bountysource.

For example, posting a {{1000 | dollars}} bounty from PayPal costs you exactly {{1000 | dollars}}.

Earning Money

Claiming a Bounty

We do not charge any fees to claim a bounty. The amount displayed as the bounty total is the amount that will be transferred to your Bountysource account.

Fundraisers & Team Contributions

We do not charge any fees on contributions to a team or a fundraiser. For example, 100% of a {{1000 | dollars}} pledge will go into the team account.

All pledges and team contributions will be available to the team as soon as the payment clears.

Withdrawals

Bountysource charges a 10% fee on all withdrawals from your Bountysource account. This includes any money earned from Bounties, Fundraisers, or Donations.

To request a cash out, please use the cash out form. We can send you money via PayPal, Bitcoin, or physical check. Bountysource is not responsible for any fees that may be incurred due to the chosen payment method.
