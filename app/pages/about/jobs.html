Technical Intern

We are seeking a Technical intern to assist in a variety of daily tasks.

Duties may include:

  • Data mining / analysis of open source projects
  • Generating reports on user activity
  • Quality Assurance testing & feedback
  • Assisting in outreach efforts

We are looking for an individual who possesses the following:

  • Passion for open-source software development
  • Good communication skills. We are a small team and thrive on communication.
  • No fear of getting hands dirty -- there will be opportunities to create and manage your own scripts, contribute to our front end, and add features to our admin.

Pluses:

  • You’ve already made a few Pull Requests on our front end
  • Intimate knowledge of GitHub
  • Existing contributor to open source software
  • Experience with the following technologies:
    • Ruby on Rails
    • JavaScript
    • Angular.js
    • Twitter Bootstrap

Based on performance and fit this position has strong potential to grow into a permanent position. This is a paid internship.

If you don’t see an opening here that suits you but you feel like you should be a part of our team, contact us anyway and tell us why we should hire you.
