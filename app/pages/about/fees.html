Fees & Payments

Payments

Payout vs. Cashout

A Payout is a transfer of money from Bountysource to a Bountysource account.

A Cashout is a withdrawal of your Bountysource balance. All cashouts are processed on a per-request basis. We can send you money via Paypal, Google Wallet, physical check, or bank wire. Bountysource is not responsible for any fees incurred due to the chosen cashout method.
To request a cashout of your current Bountysource balance, please contact us.

Bounty Payouts

Bounties are automatically paid out to a developer's Bountysource account once their solution is accepted. You can request a cashout via email.

Fundraiser Payouts

Flexible Funding

Pledges are paid out to a developer's Bountysource account as soon as the fundraiser breaches its funding goal. If the fundraiser raises more than its funding goal, a second payout occurs at the end of the fundraiser.

If the funding goal is never met, payout occurs when the fundraiser ends.

All-or-Nothing

Pledges are paid out to a developer's Bountysource account as soon as the fundraiser breaches its funding goal. If the fundraiser raises more than its funding goal, a second payout occurs at the end of the fundraiser.

Bounty Fees

Claiming a bounty

We do not charge anything to claim a bounty. The amount displayed as the bounty total is the exact amount a developer will receive upon payout.

Placing a bounty

Bountysource charges a 10% non-refundable fee for placing bounties in addition to the specified bounty amount.

For example, to place a $1,000 bounty on an issue, you will be charged $1,100.

Fundraiser Fees

Bountysource Fee

Bountysource collects a 10% non-refundable fee from all pledges.

If an all-or-nothing fundraiser's goal is not met, backers are refunded their pledged amount less the Bountysource fee.

Payment Processing Fees

Bountysource does not charge any payment processing fees. Payment processing costs are factored into our 10% fee.

Merchandise Fees

If a fundraiser creator chooses to have Bountysource fulfill merchandise, this cost will also be deducted from the funds raised. If you're considering a fundraiser and would like to have Bountysource create t-shirts, stickers, or other merchandise, contact us.
